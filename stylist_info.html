Meet Your Stylists!

Sam

From humble beginnings in Scarborough ON, Sam has been cutting hair since she was 16. A decade in the business, she specializes in cutting curly hair, braiding and natural styles.

She’s our official baker as well so enjoy a complimentary baked treat during your next visit - on her!

Mario

Born and raised here in Ottawa, Mario keeps our community looking fresh! As our head stylist, Mario excels in all of our offered services. Bringing 30 years in the business he is very wise. You are sure to learn a thing or two from him during your consultation and appointment!

Mario specializes in event bookings whether you need him for your groomsmen or bridesmaids as well!

Yara

Off the west coast, Yara joins us from Vancouver! She has experience doing hair internationally in the UAE and Singapore but now hangs with us here at The Moussalon. 15 years in the industry, she specializes in hair treatments. Whether you’re coming in for a new hair colour or a perm, Yara loves it all!
